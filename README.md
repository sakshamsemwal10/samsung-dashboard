# 📊 Samsung Mobile Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Samsung mobile sales performance across different cities, products, customer ratings, and payment methods.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and interactive filtering capabilities.

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify top-performing products and regions
- Evaluate payment method preferences
- Track sales trends over time
- Support data-driven business decision-making

## Dashboard Features

### 📍 Geographic Sales Analysis
- Visual representation of sales across major cities in India
- Helps identify high-performing markets

### 📈 Sales Trend Analysis
- Daily sales trend tracking
- Quantity sold analysis by day

### ⭐ Customer Rating Analysis
- Breakdown of customer ratings
- Helps evaluate customer satisfaction levels

### 💳 Payment Method Analysis
- Distribution of transactions across:
  - Credit Card
  - Debit Card
  - UPI
  - Cash

### 📱 Product Performance Analysis
- Mobile model-wise sales comparison
- Brand-wise sales and transaction analysis

### 🎛 Interactive Filters
Users can dynamically filter data by:
- Month
- Mobile Model
- Brand
- Payment Method
- Day Name
